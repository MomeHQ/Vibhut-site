Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Vibhut ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Vibhut mobile application (the "App"). Vibhut is published by MomeHQ (the "Company"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

If you have any questions, contact us at: hello@momehq.com

Who We Are

Vibhut is a Hindu festival, story, and tradition app designed for parents in the Indian diaspora to share their cultural heritage with their children. The App provides:

  • Festival information and dates
  • Cultural and mythological stories
  • Mantras, puja rituals, and traditions
  • Conversational AI assistance ("Mitra") for parent and child questions about Hindu culture

We are based in California, USA, and operate globally.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use Vibhut, we collect:

  • Account information: email address, password (securely hashed and stored)
  • Profile information: your name, your child's name, child's age (optional, used to personalize stories)
  • Content you create: messages you send to Mitra (the AI companion), notes or favorites within the App
  • Subscription information: when you subscribe to Vibhut Premium, we receive purchase confirmations and entitlement status from Apple App Store, Google Play Store, or RevenueCat (our subscription management partner). We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we collect:

  • Device information: device type, operating system version, app version, language preference
  • Usage information: which features you use, festival pages viewed, stories opened (used to improve the App)
  • Technical information: IP address, error reports, crash logs

We do not collect precise location data.

Information About Children

Vibhut is intended to be used by parents on behalf of their children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 (or the equivalent age in your jurisdiction).

When you provide a child's name or age in your profile, this information is used solely to personalize the App experience (e.g., addressing stories to your child by name). We do not use this information for advertising or share it with third parties for marketing.

If you believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, contact us at hello@momehq.com and we will delete it promptly.

How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's features
  • Personalize content (e.g., addressing stories to your child by name)
  • Process your AI ("Mitra") conversations and return responses
  • Manage your subscription and entitlements
  • Send you optional emails (festival reminders, monthly digest) — only if you opt in
  • Communicate with you about service updates, security, or support
  • Improve the App, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Retention

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you have rights regarding your data:

  • Access: request a copy of the data we have about you
  • Correction: correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out: unsubscribe from emails at any time

To exercise these rights, email hello@momehq.com from the email address associated with your account. We respond within 30 days.

California Residents (CCPA)

You have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt out of "sale" (we do not sell). Submit requests to hello@momehq.com.

European Residents (GDPR)

We process your data on the basis of consent (for emails), contract (to provide the App), and legitimate interests (security, improvements). You have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and object. The data controller is MomeHQ at hello@momehq.com.

Data Security

We use industry-standard practices:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest (Supabase, our database provider)
  • Hashed passwords (we cannot read your password)
  • Limited access controls within our team
  • API keys stored on secure infrastructure, never in mobile app bundles

No system is 100% secure. If a breach occurs, we will notify affected users and authorities as required by law.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate. We use providers with appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses, Data Processing Agreements).

Third-Party Links

Specific Notes for Vibhut

About Mitra (AI Companion)

Mitra uses Claude, an AI by Anthropic, to answer questions about Hindu festivals, mythology, and traditions. When you message Mitra:

  • Your message is sent to Anthropic's API to generate a response
  • Conversation history may be stored to maintain context across sessions
  • Anthropic does not use these messages to train its AI models for our account
  • Mitra is intended for cultural and educational purposes only
  • Mitra does NOT discuss politics, violence, medical advice, or content unsuitable for children

About Children's Use

If you allow your child to use the App:

  • We recommend supervising their use
  • Mitra is content-filtered to stay on cultural/festival topics
  • We do not collect data directly from children
  • The app is designed to be age-appropriate

About Subscriptions

Vibhut Premium unlocks additional features (unlimited Mitra messages, festival reminders, etc.). Subscriptions are managed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store; we receive entitlement status only. To cancel, manage your subscription through your device's app store settings.
